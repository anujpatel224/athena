Frequently asked questions

Do you provide decor services?

Yes. With over 20 years of experience, we have styled countless weddings using complete décor and theming packages. Our team would be delighted to share ideas and help bring your vision to life working with industry-leading suppliers.

Do you provide drinks packages?

Yes. To make planning effortless, we offer a range of drinks packages, including unlimited options for spirits, beers, and soft drinks, so you can relax knowing your guests are well looked after.

Can we bring our own alcohol?

Yes. You are welcome to bring your own soft drinks, wine, spirits, and champagne, with no corkage charges.

How do I confirm my booking?

To secure your booking, we require a signed contract and a deposit, as outlined in the agreement. Flexible payment terms can be arranged upon request.

Can you recommend suppliers?

Yes. Having coordinated weddings for over 20 years, we can recommend a carefully curated shortlist of trusted suppliers, including caterers, DJs, decorators, and more. You are also welcome to work with suppliers of your choice.

Can we personalise the décor and theme for our wedding?

Absolutely. The venue is highly versatile and can be transformed to suit your chosen colour palette, theme, and style, allowing you to create a wedding that feels truly personal.

Do you have accommodation on site?

Athena Events Venue does not have on-site accommodation. However, there are several hotels located within walking distance of the venue. Please contact us if you would like a recommended list.

Are there nearby hotels for wedding guests staying overnight?

Yes. Several hotels are located within walking distance of Athena Events Venue, making it convenient for guests attending the celebration.
