ATHENA IS HIRING

Our team is the most important part of what makes Athena tick. We hire dynamic and flexible people that will make clients' events utterly memorable for their guests.

Bar Staff

Enthusiastic and hardworking bar staff wanted for event days, based in Leicester's cultural quarter.

Athena hosts a variety of events ranging from Bongo's Bingo & live music events to gala dinners, awards ceremonies, weddings, and Christmas parties. As part of our dynamic bar team, you will be required to prepare drinks to a high standard and with exceptional service.

We want you to be proud of the service you deliver with a willingness to learn new skills and develop the ones you already have.

Successful candidates must; enjoy working behind a busy bar, have fantastic communication skills, be friendly and presentable, have the right to work in the UK and must be over 18.

Experience is preferred but not necessary, training is provided for the right candidate.

• Job Type: Part-time, Permanent

• Salary: £9.60 per hour

Cleaners & Venue Set Up Assistants

Hardworking individuals are required to assist with the set-up and dismantling of event setups.

Duties include moving and setting up tables, chairs, linen, hoovering, cleaning and general venue tasks as required.

Involves lifting and carrying - must be physically fit. Full training provided.

Maintaining equipment inventories/ counts.

Flexible shifts are available ranging from days, evenings and overnight subject to event needs.

You will be part of a dynamic hard-working team with great progression opportunities.

• Job Type: Part-time

• Salary: £11.44 per hour
