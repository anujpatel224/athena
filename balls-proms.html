Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the venue for all guests and event attendees.

What are the dimensions of rooms & are there cloakroom facilities available?

Our main cinema suite measures 29.5m x 28.8m, with additional spaces including the Gallery, Deco Lounge, and City View areas. Cloakroom facilities are available for your guests' convenience.
