Frequently asked questions

What capacity does Athena Leicester offer for awards and gala dinners?

Athena Leicester can host awards and dinner events for audiences ranging from intimate gatherings to large-scale celebrations, with flexible seating and stage configurations. For dining, we can accommodate 150 to 900 guests, including the balcony.

Can the venue be styled to match our brand or theme?

Yes. Our spaces are highly adaptable and can be transformed to reflect your brand identity, colour palette, or event theme.

Is there a dedicated stage and AV setup for award presentations?

Absolutely. Athena offers professional staging, lighting, sound, and screen options suitable for speeches, presentations, and live entertainment.

Do you have on-site catering?

Yes. Athena Leicester is supported by an experienced, award-winning team of in-house chefs who specialise in fresh, flavoursome cuisine. All dietary requirements and allergies can be catered to.

Do you allow external caterers for awards dinners?

We understand that you may have preferred caterers. External catering is permitted, subject to the provision of all required health and safety and insurance documentation. We also kindly request that the kitchen be left in the same condition as it was found.

Is the venue suitable for formal, black-tie events?

Yes. Athena's Art Deco interiors provide an elegant backdrop ideal for black-tie and premium awards evenings.

Is the guest arrival and registration space available?

Our foyer and reception areas are designed to accommodate guest check-ins, welcome drinks, and networking before the main event.

Are rehearsal and setup times included?

Yes, advance setup and rehearsal access can be arranged to ensure a smooth event experience.

Is Athena Leicester suitable for corporate gala dinners?

Yes. Athena Leicester is well-suited for corporate gala dinners, offering an elegant Art Deco setting that can be tailored for formal dining, award presentations, and evening entertainment.
