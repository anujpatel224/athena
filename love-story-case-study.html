A LOVE STORY BROUGHT TO LIFEAT ATHENA LEICESTER

From breathtaking décor to seamless coordination, Athena became more than just a venue — it was the heartbeat of a celebration filled with colour, joy, and togetherness.

A Celebration of Love: Exaucee & Senior's Wedding at Athena Leicester

Weddings Friday, 22nd August 2025

Exaucee and Senior chose Athena Leicester to host their wedding—a day that brought together family and friends in a celebration of love, carefully planned and beautifully delivered with the support of the Athena team.

From the first meeting to the final farewell, Athena’s combination of peace of mind, hands-on support, and Art Deco grandeur allowed the couple to focus on what mattered most: enjoying their day.

Bringing the Vision to Life

Exaucee and Senior had a clear vision for their wedding, and our team worked alongside them—and with their chosen suppliers—to bring it to life. Whether working with Athena’s in-house kitchen or coordinating with external caterers, every detail was aligned with their wishes. The result was a seamless blend of stunning décor, delicious food, and a room that felt entirely their own.

Planning meetings, menu tastings, and layout decisions were all handled with care, so that by the time the big day arrived, the couple could relax and enjoy every moment.

The Big Day

On Friday, 22nd August 2025, 300 guests took their seats in the main hall, with 50 more on the balcony, for a ceremony and reception that filled Athena with joy. The scale of the venue allowed for a grand yet intimate atmosphere, while our team ensured that every transition—from ceremony to drinks, dinner to dancing—ran smoothly behind the scenes.

From coordination with photographers and videographers to managing timings and guest flow, Athena’s experience in hosting weddings meant that Exaucee and Senior could focus entirely on each other and their guests.

A Bride's Words of Gratitude

In her glowing five-star Google review, Exaucee shared:

The team at Athena were so friendly and helpful from start to finish. They went above and beyond to make our day perfect, and we couldn’t have asked for more. We also saved thousands compared to other venues we looked at—without compromising on anything. We’d recommend Athena to any couple looking for a stunning, stress-free wedding.

Why Athena Leicester?

Athena offers couples peace of mind, dedicated support, and an Art Deco setting that needs little extra decoration to feel special. Our aim is to deliver exceptional service so that your wedding day is exactly what you imagine—and Exaucee and Senior’s celebration was a reflection of everything Athena stands for.

As Exaucee put it: “If I had known how incredible the Athena team was, I wouldn’t have hired my own coordinator.” We were honoured to play a part in their love story and to help bring their vision to life under our roof.
