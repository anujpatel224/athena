Cirque-Inspired Post Peak Party at Athena Leicester

Amazon EMA1 Post Peak Party at Athena Leicester

Awards & Dinners 8th & 9th March 2023

Amazon EMA1 brought over 1000 team members to Athena Leicester for their post peak party—a Cirque du Soleil–inspired celebration that turned the venue into a world of performers, street food, and non-stop entertainment.

With Athena’s Art Deco setting as the backdrop, the evening featured fire eaters, magicians, jesters, ballet, and aerialists. Guests enjoyed a buffet-style dinner with street food stations, while an on-account bar and a packed dancefloor kept the energy high. Jaw-dropping production and light shows ran throughout the night.

Event organiser Claire Haye worked closely with Athena’s team—including Hira Ram and her colleagues—to plan and deliver the event from December through to February, with seamless execution on the night.

Event Highlights

  • Over 1000 guests celebrated at Athena Leicester
  • Street food stations and buffet-style dinner
  • Cirque du Soleil theme with fire eaters, magicians, jesters, ballet, and aerialists
  • On-account bar
  • Packed dancefloor
  • Jaw-dropping production and light shows throughout the evening
  • Seamless planning and execution from December to February

Client Feedback

“Just like that EMA1’s Post Peak Party is over. Great night with talented performers and The Athena in Leicester was a fantastic venue thanks to Hira Ram and her team. I love organising this event as it’s a great way to recognise and celebrate the amazing team at Amazon.”

– Claire Haye, Amazon EMA1
