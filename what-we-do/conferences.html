Athena is the Midlands' conference venue with a twist

Available for large scale conferences, open space meetings, and product launches, Athena is an inspired choice for companies looking to escape the humdrum of the boardroom or conference room.

Our state of the art AV and presentation technology, dedicated leased fibre connectivity, unique and flexible space, alongside our award winning catering will make sure that your conference delegates will be truly engaged and ready to be well informed.

For more information about choosing Athena as your venue, please call 0116 2626556 or email contact@athenacb.co.uk.  

A TRULY FLEXIBLE CONFERENCE SPACE

Our facilities are a change from the usual stark, plain and drab business conference venues and offer a unique setting for your conference, AGM or product launch. Watch our Video Tour for a flavour of the conference venue.

We can cater for small groups and large delegations alike, and our superb conference set-up means that your delegates will be ready to be engaged and well-informed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I provisionally book the venue?
Yes, you can hold a date provisionally while you finalize the arrangements. Contact us via phone or email for further details.
Can I get WiFi at the venue?
Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the venue.
What are the dimensions of rooms & are there cloakroom facilities available?
The rooms vary in size to accommodate different events. Cloakroom facilities are available for guests.
Can we set up the day before the event?
Yes, early setup can be arranged based on availability. Please coordinate with our team in advance.
Can I view Athena?
Absolutely! You can schedule a guided tour of Athena before making a booking.
Do you have AV on site?
We have an extensive AV in-house system, including projectors, sound systems, and lighting. Contact us for the latest specifications.
Can we bring our own AV?
Yes, you are welcome to bring your own AV equipment. Our team can assist with setup if needed.
Do you have on-site catering?
Yes, we offer a variety of catering options, including buffets and plated meals.
