Located in the heart of Leicester City's cultural quarter, Athena is the perfect location for your exhibition or festival.

Athena is a large blank canvas with an added bonus of being in an historical art deco building. With events such as Comic Con and The Gin Festival returning to the venue year on year, it's no surprise when Athena comes equipped with all of the modern AV and technical expertise you would expect from a leading event space. With a convenient city centre location, excellent transport links, onsite car parking and within walking distance from a number of hotels, Athena has everything you will need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I provisionally book the venue?
Yes, you can hold a date provisionally while you finalize the arrangements. Contact us via phone or email for further details.
Can I get WiFi at the venue?
Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the venue.
What are the dimensions of rooms & are there cloakroom facilities available?
The rooms vary in size to accommodate different events. Cloakroom facilities are available for guests.
Can we set up the day before the event?
Yes, early setup can be arranged based on availability. Please coordinate with our team in advance.
Can I view Athena?
Absolutely! You can schedule a guided tour of Athena before making a booking.
Can we bring our own catering?
Yes, you are welcome to bring your own catering. Our team will be happy to assist with logistics and setup to ensure everything runs smoothly.
Can we bring our own AV?
Yes, you are welcome to bring your own AV equipment. Our team can assist with setup if needed.
Do you have on-site catering?
Yes, we offer a variety of catering options, including buffets and plated meals.
