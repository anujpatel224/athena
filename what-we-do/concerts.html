CONCERTS CONCERTS CONCERTS CONCERTS

Athena is a great choice of venue for concerts, classical and modern live music events , diasporic/world music shows, as well as improvisational theatre and professional comedy nights

With a capacity of up to 2000 guests, 5 bars across 3 levels, the impressive main auditorium has an uninterrupted view to the purpose-built stage with gallery viewing over.

We offer an impressive specification of in house AV equipment with full technical support. Ranging from state of the art moving heads to a 10m cinema screen, star cloth walling, line-array speakers, extensive lighting and an experienced operations team, Athena gives your concert an amazing foundation for success.
