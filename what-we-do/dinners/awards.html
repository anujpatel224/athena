The finest venue the industry has to offer

Whether you want an elegant celebration dinner or a lavish awards dinner, Athena will make the night truly special, where only the best will do and only the best is provided.

From outstanding catering and hospitality to impressive in-house AV and technical support, Athena is highly acclaimed within the events industry. You can be sure that alongside our experienced and dedicated team we will help you to create the amazing night that your guests deserve.
